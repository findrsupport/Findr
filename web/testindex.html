Explore Real Estate in Canada

REW offers the best real estate search experience for individuals and families looking for new properties in Canada. We're a leading real estate platform with access to MLS® listings across Canada's thriving housing market. Whether you're searching for a home to rent or a property for sale , you'll find countless options to choose from.

Canada offers numerous advantages for experienced and first-time homebuyers, with a strong and stable economy, a diverse population, and a high quality of life. From bustling urban centers to charming rural towns, there 's something for everyone in the Canadian housing market.

Ways to Browse MLS® Listings

REW's MLS® property search makes it easy to narrow down your options based on location, price, and other important factors and if you prefer a more visual way to browse properties you can also try our MLS® listings map . With our user-friendly platform, you can easily find preconstruction homes for sale, condos, land you might be interested in, and other properties that meet your specific criteria. You can even find investment properties such as duplexes and larger multi-family homes. If you're more interested in rentals rather than buying a home, REW also has a robust list of residential properties to rent across Canada. Our MLS® listings are continuously updated on our platform to ensure you see the newest listings as they arrive on the market.

If you need help on your real estate journey, we have a list of real estate agents in your area that can help you to find the right property for your specific needs and budget. Our mortgage specialists are also able to secure the best mortgage rate for your home purchase. Buying a home is a big decision and a major milestone in the lives of many Canadians. Whether it's your first home or you're a seasoned home buyer, REW has everything you need to get you started on your real estate adventure. Take advantage of the opportunity to find your ideal home in the vibrant and dynamic world of Canadian real estate.
